About Us

We're more than just a drink brand - we're your daily dose of energy,health,and community. Every sip you take is made from the freshest ingredients,mixed with care, and served with love.Whether you're here for a boost, a detox, or just a reason to smile, The Doormans is here to open that door for you.

The Doormans started with one simple idea:

Healthy drinks should be for everyone.

We saw a gap - too many sugar-packed, artificial drinks out there and not enough options that keep you healthy and taste amazing. So we crafted a line of herbal,fruity,and energizing drinks designed to fuel your body and mind.

We're also future driven - using Web3 and digital tools to give our customers rewards,community acess, and even collectible drink experiences
